# 📁 Projects Included

## 1️⃣ ATM Transactions Analysis

An **interactive Power BI dashboard** analyzing ATM transaction patterns across different cities and states in India.

### 🔍 Key Highlights
- Interactive dashboard built with **Power BI**
- Analysis of **ATM usage patterns**
- Identification of **high-load ATM locations**
- Evaluation of **profitability and operational costs**

### 📈 Insights Generated
- Some regions have **high transactions despite fewer ATMs**
- Certain ATMs generate **higher revenue through financial transactions**
- Loss-making ATMs identified through **margin analysis**
- Profit distribution follows the **Pareto Principle (80/20 rule)**

### 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Excel  
- DAX  
- Data Visualization  

📂 **Project Folder:** `ATM_Transactions_Analysis`

## 2️⃣ Super Store Sales Dashboard & Forecast

A **Power BI sales analytics project** analyzing a retail store dataset and forecasting future sales trends.

### 🔍 Key Highlights
- Sales performance analysis across **regions, categories, and segments**
- **Interactive visualizations** for business insights
- **Time series forecasting** for sales planning
- KPI monitoring for sales, profit, and quantity

### 📈 Business Insights
- Consumer segment generates the **largest revenue share**
- **California leads in total sales**
- Online payment methods are increasingly preferred
- Office Supplies is the **top performing category**

### 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Data Modeling  
- Business Analytics  
- Time Series Forecasting  

📂 **Project Folder:** `SuperStore_Sales_Dashboard`
